Revision Control
ISO 9001:2015 Compliance

This revision tracking system satisfies ISO 9001:2015 clause 7.5 (Documented Information Control) by maintaining a controlled record of drawing revisions with effectivity dates.

Superseding Revisions

When a new revision supersedes previous ones, the system automatically marks older revisions as "Superseded" with the effective date of the new revision.

Work Order Linking

Work orders will be linked to specific part revisions, ensuring full traceability of which drawing version was used for each production run.
